## Workshop Programme

The workshop will take place on *Wednesday, 15 April, starting at 2:15PM in P1 - Room 123*.

The schedule of activities is organised into 2 sessions, each of 90 minutes duration with a coffee break at 3:45-4:30pm.

**Part 1 — Introduction (30 minutes)**
 - Welcome & intrdocution to the workshop
 - Ice-breaker Activity: Participants are invited to imagine the future of a digital world free of violence and select a "visual card" that best represents this vision.
 - Speed Introductions: Meet another participant and take a minute each to: *Introduce yourself. Why are you interested in this workshop? Why do you pick this visual card? What do you want to get from the workshop at the end?* (change partners when prompted – after 2-3 minutes)

 **Part 2 - Concepts of Responsibility & Responsible Design (60 minutes)**
  - Introducing card-sorting: Using generic deck of drinking vessels, demonstrate the card sorting activity and what should be recorded for each sort
  - Concept exploration – 2 people in each group, use card sorting to explore each of three concepts – gender-based online harm, responsibility, responsible design. Please sort the following cards based on your own criteria and categories, share with your group; you may sort the cards differently based on two or more criteria. Take turns to group the cards reflecting on the groups and sorting criteria you use in each case. Facilitators will record each sort by photographing it.
  - After 4-5 sorts have been completed (or 10 minutes have elapsed), convene a plenary discussion and invite pairs to their interesting and/or surprising groups and criteria.
  - Wrap up session with a meta-discussion / reflection on the method

  **COFFEE BREAK**

**Part 3 - Imaginaries of Responsible Design for Mitigating Online Harms Against Women & Girls (60 minutes)**
 - Regroup participants to 3-4 people as a group, select and refine a “How might we …” scenario to focus on – 5 min 
 - Create a possibility map – 30 min
 - Gallery Walk & Synthesis – 15 min 
 - Quick Share-Out – 10 min
  
**Part 4 – Wrap up (30 minutes)**
 - Key highlights / takeaways from the possibility maps (15 mins) – 
   - over what time frame could these possibilities be realised – immediate, near, distant futures? 
   - what research questions do we need to address to realise these possibilities?
 - Meta discussion / reflection on methods (5 minutes)
 - Future plans (10 mins) – 
   - What would you like to do next?  Next week, next month, next year?
   - How would you like to contribute to output / paper from this workshop?
   - Are there particular research questions you’d like to work on together?

This work is supported by the [Centre for Protecting Women Online](https://cpwo.open.ac.uk), at The Open University, UK.
